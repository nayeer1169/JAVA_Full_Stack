Favorite Movie of All Time

★ 9.2 IMDb Biographical Drama Filmfare Best Film

12th Fail

Directed by: Vidhu Vinod Chopra Starring: Vikrant Massey, Medha Shankar Release: 2023

Based on the true story of IPS officer Manoj Kumar Sharma, 12th Fail chronicles his journey from a small village in Chambal, where he failed his 12th-grade boards, to conquering the toughest civil service exam in India. It is a raw, emotional, and deeply moving portrayal of determination, integrity, and absolute resilience.
